Learn Crypto (Easy Examples)

1. What is Crypto?

Crypto is digital money stored in a wallet. Example: If 1 BTC = $40,000, buying $100 gives you 0.0025 BTC.

2. What is a Wallet?

A wallet is like a banking app. Example: Your wallet shows 0.05 BTC and 2.3 SOL.

3. What is Blockchain?

A public notebook of transactions. Example: Sending 0.01 BTC gives a trackable Transaction ID.

4. What are Gas Fees?

Small fees to process transactions. Example: ETH = $2, Solana = $0.001.

5. What Are Stablecoins?

Coins that never change value. Example: 1 USDT = 1 USD.

6. Why Businesses Use Crypto?

  • Fast (5–10 sec)
  • Lower fees
  • International payments

Example: USDT invoice payments don’t fluctuate.
