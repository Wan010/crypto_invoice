Why Accept Crypto?

Faster cross-border payments

Crypto transfers can be substantially faster than bank wires, especially cross-border.

Lower fees for stablecoins

Stablecoins (USDC/USDT) on certain chains can be cheaper than international bank fees.

New customer segments

Accepting crypto opens access to a global user base and Web3 native customers.

Getting started

  1. Create a secure wallet (Metamask, Trust Wallet)
  2. Understand on-chain confirmations
  3. Decide whether to hold crypto or convert to fiat
